## Tập 105 — Can You Loop Me In?

**Slug:** `can-you-loop-me-in` · **Grammar:** get looped in · get pinged · get up to speed

### Ngữ pháp (tiếng Việt)

1. **get looped in on + topic** = được thêm vào kênh/email/meeting. Sai: *get loop in* (thiếu -ed).
2. **get pinged** = bị tag/nhắn liên tục trên Slack.
3. **get up to speed on + topic** = nắm bắt nhanh tình hình.

| Panel | Dialogue |
|-------|----------|
| 1 | PM: *"Welcome! I'll get you looped in on the launch channel."* |
| 2 | Nam: *"Sorry — I keep getting pinged on every thread."* |
| 3 | Buddy: *"Don't worry. We'll get you up to speed by standup tomorrow."* |
| 4 | Nam: *"There are twelve stakeholders I wasn't looped in on yet."* |
| 5 | Nam: *"I'm getting up to speed — but I still get pinged after hours."* PM: *"That's startup life."* |
| 6 | **NEXT:** We Need Buy-In |

## Tổng quan arc

Nam nhận email recruiter sau khi team công nhận performance. Arc này dạy **behavioral interview (STAR)**, **career pitch**, **salary negotiation nhẹ**, và **questions for interviewer** — khác hẳn debug meeting.

## Tập 80 — Tell Me About a Time When...

| | |
|---|---|
| **Slug** | `tell-me-about-a-time-when` |
| **Setting** | Video interview, whiteboard STAR (Situation-Task-Action-Result) |
| **Mục tiêu học** | Trả lời behavioral bằng STAR |

### Kịch bản 6 panel

| Panel | Hình ảnh | Dialogue |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Interviewer chuyên nghiệp | Interviewer: *"Tell me about a time when you handled a production incident."* |
| 2 | Nam nhớ lại payment outage (flashback nhỏ) | Nam: *"One challenge I faced was a payment outage before a demo."* |
| 3 | Nam giải thích action | Nam: *"I walked the team through the logs and we rolled back safely."* |
| 4 | Result | Nam: *"The result was that we saved the demo and added better monitoring."* |
| 5 | Lesson | Nam: *"What I learned from that was to always run through the main flow early."* |
| 6 | Interviewer gật | Interviewer: *"Strong example — clear structure."* |

**ENGLISH FOCUS:** Tell me about a time when… / One challenge I faced was… / What I learned from that was…

**Hook:** Next: Why Do You Want This Role?
